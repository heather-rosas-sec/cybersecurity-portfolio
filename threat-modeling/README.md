
# Threat Modeling & Executive Summary

This project demonstrates threat modeling and risk analysis skills developed through cybersecurity training. It focuses on identifying potential threats, assessing risk impact, and communicating findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders through an executive summary.

## Key Focus Areas
- Asset identification and attack surface analysis
- Threat identification and prioritization
- Risk assessment and impact evaluation
- Mitigation considerations
- Executive-level communication and reporting

## Notes
This project is based on a training scenario and does not include real organizational data.
